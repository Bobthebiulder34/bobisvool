Republic of Cheth: The Ivory Senate
Race: Primarily High Elves.

Geography: Coastal cities with grand ivory towers and advanced arcane institutions.

Culture: Highly intellectual and arcane-focused, governed by the Ivory Senate.

Relations: Cheth serves as the diplomatic and magical powerhouse within the alliance with Kud and Deloragh. They advocate for expansion and take a more aggressive stance against Dhod, viewing the Dragonborns as a potential threat to elven prosperity.
