Protectorate of Deloragh: Guardians of the Wild
Race: Predominantly Wood Elves, with fey and druidic communities.

Geography: Dense forests and moors with strong connections to nature and the Feywild.

Culture: Deeply connected to nature, Deloragh focuses on protecting the wild and maintaining harmony with the land.

Relations: Deloragh is a reluctant member of the Elven Alliance, preferring peace and neutrality but honor-bound to support Kud if war with Dhod arises.
