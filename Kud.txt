Kud: The Elven Realm
Race: Predominantly High Elves and Wood Elves.
Geography: Lush forests and rivers, with architecture blending into nature.
Culture: Focused on magic, art, and diplomacy, with strong ties to nature.
Relations: Kud has a long-standing rivalry with Dhod, leading to historical conflicts. Currently allied with the Republic of Cheth and the Protectorate of Deloragh due to their shared Elven heritage.
